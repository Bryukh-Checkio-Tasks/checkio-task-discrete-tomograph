Sophia found the old bunker. The bunker has the square form -- the 9 levels with 9 rooms in each. But some rooms were mined and Sophie wants to explore the location of rooms with bombs. Nicola has built the special scanner which allow to count bombs in each level and column of rooms.

The robots had scanned all rows and columns and got two line of numbers -- the quantity of bombs in each level from up to down and in each column from left to right as strings. These data are insufficient for the bunker's map. But we can attempt to check the control room. Besides scan data we know the coordinates of the control room. You should

The map of the bunker should be represented as a list of strings, where clear rooms are shown as "-" and danger rooms as "X".

Input: Two arguments. A quantity of bombs in each level from top to down and in each column from left to right, strings.

Output: The map of the bunker as a list of strings with "X" and "-".
